# 03_副詞子句

## 核心概念
副詞子句 (Adverb Clause) 是由「從屬連接詞 + 完整子句」構成，在句子中扮演「副詞」的角色，修飾主句的動詞，說明時間、原因、條件、對比、目的、結果等關係。多益最常考「連接詞意思的辨別」以及「連接詞 vs. 介系詞的詞性判斷」。

## 1. 常見副詞子句連接詞分類 🌟

### 時間 (When)
| 連接詞 | 中文 | 範例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **when** | 當...時 | *When* the meeting **ends**, I will call you. |
| **while** | 當...的同時 | *While* he **was presenting**, I took notes. |
| **before** | 在...之前 | Please review the report *before* you **submit** it. |
| **after** | 在...之後 | *After* the project **was completed**, they celebrated. |
| **as soon as** | 一...就... | I will inform you *as soon as* a decision **is made**. |
| **until** | 直到...為止 | The office will be closed *until* renovations **are done**. |
| **by the time** | 到了...的時候 | *By the time* you **arrive**, the meeting will have started. |

> [!warning] 時間副詞子句不用未來式
> 在時間副詞子句中，用**現在式**代替未來式！
> - ❌ When he **will arrive**, I will tell him.
> - ✅ When he **arrives**, I will tell him.

### 原因 (Because)
| 連接詞 | 中文 | 範例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **because** | 因為 (最強調原因) | *Because* the deadline **was moved**, we worked overtime. |
| **since** | 由於 (已知原因) | *Since* you **are** the expert, please guide us. |
| **as** | 因為 (語氣較弱) | *As* the office **was** full, the meeting was moved. |
| **now that** | 既然 (現在已經...) | *Now that* the issue **has been resolved**, we can proceed. |

### 條件 (If)
| 連接詞 | 中文 | 範例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **if** | 如果 | *If* you **have** any questions, please contact us. |
| **unless** | 除非 (= if not) | *Unless* you **submit** the form, you won't be registered. |
| **provided (that)** | 只要 (條件較嚴格) | *Provided that* all conditions **are met**, the deal is final. |
| **as long as** | 只要 | You can use the lab *as long as* you **follow** the rules. |

> [!tip]
> `unless` = `if ... not`，不要雙重否定！
> - ❌ Unless you *don't* submit... (錯誤)
> - ✅ Unless you *submit*... (正確)

### 對比/讓步 (Although)
| 連接詞 | 中文 | 範例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **although / though / even though** | 雖然、儘管 | *Although* the budget **was tight**, the project succeeded. |
| **while / whereas** | 然而、相較於 (表對比) | Sales *increased*, *while/whereas* costs *decreased*. |

### 目的 (So that)
| 連接詞 | 中文 | 範例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **so that** | 為了 (目的) | She worked overtime *so that* the team **could meet** the deadline. |
| **in order that** | 為了 (正式) | We upgraded the system *in order that* users **can** access it faster. |

## 2. 連接詞 vs. 介系詞 (多益重點比較) 🌟
請參考 [[04_連接詞]] 中的對照表格。

| 語意 | **連接詞** (後接完整子句 S+V) | **介系詞** (後接名詞/V-ing) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 雖然/儘管 | although, even though | despite, in spite of |
| 因為 | because, since | because of, due to, owing to |
| 直到 | until | until (可接名詞，也可接子句) |

## 📝 多益秒殺解題技巧
1.  **判斷空格後方的結構**：
    *   後面是**完整子句 (S+V)** ➔ 選**連接詞** (although, because, since, when...)。
    *   後面是**名詞或 V-ing** ➔ 選**介系詞** (despite, because of, due to...)。
2.  看到 `unless` ➔ 子句不加 not，`unless` 本身已含否定。
3.  **時間副詞子句**：看到 when/before/after/as soon as 引導的子句，若主句是未來式，子句用**現在式**（不用 will）。
4.  看到 `while/whereas` 後面子句意思與主句相反/對比 ➔ 這是「對比連接詞」，而非「當...時」。
